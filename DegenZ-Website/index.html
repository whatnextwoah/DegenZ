Our Story

DegenZ started off as a small WhatsApp group with 7 people, eventually the chat became open to the world and as more people joined, we saw the impact it had on their lives so we decided to expand even further and we created a discord and twitter in the October of 2021.

We have over 3000 members in our Discord server.

We have made 2 million dollars profit from alpha calls and whitelist opportunities.

We have collaborated with over 100 projects on the ETH and Sol networks.

DegenZ DAO Member Benefits

  • Learning Resources: DegenZ has a wide range of study guides and resources on every cryptocurrency network curated by some of the brightest minds in the space.
  • Alpha Calls: We have a dedicated team of specialised NFT traders who review projects and bring our members the latest trading information and updates from. multiple networks
  • Whitelist Opportunities: DegenZ members enjoy accest to whitelist opportuinites from projects by our collaborators.
  • Futures trading and DeFi: We have a team of experienced traders who share signals to our members.
  • Trading Bots: We offer access to several trading bots to further facilitate the NFT trading experience of our users.
  • Crpto News: We have bots set to to bring the most important of news to you from all over the crypto world.
